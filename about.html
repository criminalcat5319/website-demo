Our History

Founded in 1983, Proton became Malaysia’s first national car manufacturer. The brand focuses on innovation, reliability and modern automotive design.

Innovation

Through collaboration with global automotive partners, Proton integrates advanced technology, safety systems and smart driving features.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To become a leading automotive brand that delivers innovative, high quality vehicles for customers around the world.

Mission

  • Develop advanced automotive technology
  • Provide safe and reliable vehicles
  • Ensure customer satisfaction
  • Expand to global automotive markets
